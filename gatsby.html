My sister Brooke chose to see this musical for her eighteenth birthday, and never in my life have I been more happy with her decisions. The Great Gatsby on Broadway was nothing short of spectacular, with a cast that brought the iconic story to life in an unforgettable way. They perfectly embodied the enigmatic Jay Gatsby with a mix of charm and mystery, making every moment on stage feel intense and real. The set design was a showstopper, with lavish 1920s decadence and sweeping jazz-filled moments transporting the audience into the glamour of the Roaring Twenties. The ensemble cast was equally compelling, each character bringing depth and complexity. The show captures the opulence and tragedy of Fitzgerald's novel, offering a fresh, immersive experience that resonates with both newcomers and fans of the original. Things like the actual moving cars and Gatsby’s actual roll off the stage into the orchestra pit made it hard to remember that it was a play. I adore this show and am eternally grateful to my sister for choosing to see this show on her birthday.
