It's Showtime

When I saw the show for my eleventh birthday,shortly after it's release, with it's original cast I was hooked. The show was absolutely amazing and the actors unbelievably incredible. Alex Brightman’s performance as the title character stealing the show. His wild energy and sharp humor, with the iconic Beetlejuice voice, "puppet show" bit, and interactive dialogue and not child-friendly references. In retrospect, an eleven year old girl was probably not the typical target audience but I was not a typical person.
